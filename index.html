The Marine Energy Environmental Toolkit for Permitting and Licensing for permitting and licensing effort seeks to increase regulators' understanding of marine energy projects, including from waves, tides, rivers, and ocean currents, and their potential environmental effects to reduce the amount of time to permit and decrease costs to develop marine energy projects.
